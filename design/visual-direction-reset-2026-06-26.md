# Visual Direction Reset: 云上心事

## Status

Design is rejected. Dev remains blocked.

This file is not a design spec and must not unlock test-plan or dev. It exists to reset visual exploration after two failed internal attempts.

## Why The Previous Attempts Failed

- They solved structure before taste.
- They looked generated, not art-directed.
- They treated "not a utility menu" as enough, but the product needs to feel desirable and emotionally precise.
- The bottom shell, card previews, typography, and screen density still felt internally assembled rather than designed.

## Non-Negotiable Product Bar

The next design must feel like a refined emotional travel-memory product that a real user might open again after the first card.

Hard no-go:

- Engineering wireframe feeling.
- Generic tab bar or feature menu.
- Dark panel plus beige cards as the whole visual idea.
- Overly decorative postcard nostalgia that feels old, heavy, or fake.
- Any screen that PM / PMO would hesitate to show to a real user.

## Keep From Existing Product Direction

- Expression first: user can write one sentence before flight verification.
- Verification later: flight proof gates same-flight social actions, not private writing.
- Four-entry shell can remain as an IA hypothesis: `今天 / 飞行册 / 写 / 发现`.
- `写` remains the product center.
- Cloud Card remains the shareable object.

## Explore Exactly Three Visual Directions

### Direction A: Quiet Travel Journal

Light-first, paper-led, restrained iOS-native mood. The product feels closer to a premium personal journal for flights than a dark aviation app.

Key test: does the home screen make writing feel easy, private, and calm?

### Direction B: Collectible Cloud Card

The Cloud Card becomes the hero object. The app shell is almost invisible; screens feel like a small gallery / studio around collectible cards.

Key test: does the user want to generate and keep the first card?

### Direction C: Cabin Ritual

More cinematic and ritual-like: cabin light, boarding moment, one clear sentence prompt. Fewer controls, stronger first-screen emotion.

Key test: does the user understand "before flying, leave one sentence" in three seconds?

## Gate Before Any More Screens

Only one selected direction may proceed to DESIGN_SPEC.

Before writing a new completed design manifest:

1. Show the three visual direction images directly in chat.
2. PM rejects weak directions before founder review.
3. PMO rejects anything that still looks like a generated UI board.
4. Founder chooses or rejects a direction.
5. Only then expand to Today / Flight Book / Write / Discover.
